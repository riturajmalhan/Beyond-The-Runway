About us

Since 1982, Beyond the Runway(BTR) has earned a reputation for discovering and developing models for local and international markets. It was founded by Ritu Raj Malhan and Moon Choi based in Vancouver. Their own experience as international models and the relationships they have built over the years with top clients and agents around the world have helped them build one of the premier modelling agencies in Canada. Known for his honesty and straight-forward style, Richard has earned a trustworthy name in the business. To this day these are the values on which we pride ourselves. This legacy is continued by Rya Berkelaar, a former BTR’s model with over 20+ years of experience in the industry as a model, booker and manager, having helped develop careers of some of Canada’s most successful models.
